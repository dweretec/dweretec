<p><b>Djibouti</b> (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span lang="ar" xml:lang="ar"><b><big>جيبوتي</big></b></span> ‎ <i>Jībūtī</i>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Somali_language" title="Somali language">Somali</a>: <span lang="so" xml:lang="so"><i>Jabuuti</i></span>), officially the <b>Republic of Djibouti</b>, is a country in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Horn_of_Africa" title="Horn of Africa">Horn of Africa</a>. It is bordered by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eritrea" title="Eritrea">Eritrea</a> in the north, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a> in the west and south, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Somalia" title="Somalia">Somalia</a> in the southeast. The remainder of the border is formed by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Sea" title="Red Sea">Red Sea</a> and the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf_of_Aden" title="Gulf of Aden">Gulf of Aden</a>. On the other side of the Red Sea, on the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabian_Peninsula" title="Arabian Peninsula">Arabian Peninsula</a>, 20&#160;kilometres (12.4&#160;mi) from the coast of Djibouti, is <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yemen" title="Yemen">Yemen</a>. Djibouti's size is just over 23,000&#160;square kilometres (8,900&#160;sq&#160;mi) with an estimated population of over 500,000. Its capital is the city of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Djibouti_(city)" title="Djibouti (city)">Djibouti</a>. About a fifth of the population of the country live below the international poverty line of US$1.25 a day.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Djibouti#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
