<p>A <b>loan</b> is a type of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Debt" title="Debt">debt</a>. Like all debt instruments, a loan entails the redistribution of financial <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Asset" title="Asset">assets</a> over time, between the <a href="http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/lender" class="extiw" title="wiktionary:lender">lender</a> and the <a href="http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/borrower" class="extiw" title="wiktionary:borrower">borrower</a>.</p>
<p>In a loan, the borrower initially receives or <i>borrows</i> an amount of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Money" title="Money">money</a>, called the <i>principal</i>, from the lender, and is obligated to <i>pay back</i> or <i>repay</i> an equal amount of money to the lender at a later time. Typically, the money is paid back in regular <i>installments</i>, or partial repayments; in an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Annuity_(finance_theory)" title="Annuity (finance theory)">annuity</a>, each installment is the same amount. The loan is generally provided at a cost, referred to as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interest" title="Interest">interest</a> on the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Debt" title="Debt">debt</a>, which provides an incentive for the lender to engage in the loan. In a legal loan, each of these obligations and restrictions is enforced by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Contract" title="Contract">contract</a>, which can also place the borrower under additional restrictions known as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Loan_covenant" title="Loan covenant">loan covenants</a>. Although this article focuses on monetary loans, in practice any material object might be lent.</p>
<p>Acting as a provider of loans is one of the principal tasks for <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Financial_institution" title="Financial institution">financial institutions</a>. For other institutions, issuing of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Debt" title="Debt">debt</a> contracts such as <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bond_(finance)" title="Bond (finance)">bonds</a> is a typical source of funding.</p>
