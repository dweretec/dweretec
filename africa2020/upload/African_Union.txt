<p>The <b>African Union</b> (abbreviated <b>AU</b> in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a>, and <b>UA</b> in its other official languages) is an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intergovernmental_organization" title="Intergovernmental organization">intergovernmental organization</a> consisting of 52&#160;<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">African</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sovereign_state" title="Sovereign state">states</a>. Established on <span class="mw-formatted-date" title="2002-07-09"><span class="mw-formatted-date" title="07-09"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/July_9" title="July 9">July 9</a></span>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2002" title="2002">2002</a></span>,<sup id="cite_ref-African_Union_2002_2-0" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/African_Union#cite_note-African_Union_2002-2"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> the AU was formed as a successor to the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Organization_of_African_Unity" title="Organization of African Unity" class="mw-redirect">Organization of African Unity</a> (OAU). The most important decisions of the AU are made by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assembly_of_the_African_Union" title="Assembly of the African Union">Assembly of the African Union</a>, a semi-annual meeting of the heads of state and government of its member states. The AU's secretariat, the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/African_Union_Commission" title="African Union Commission">African Union Commission</a>, is based in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Addis_Ababa" title="Addis Ababa">Addis Ababa</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a>. During the February 2009 Union meeting headed by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya" title="Libya">Libyan</a> leader <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaddafi" title="Gaddafi" class="mw-redirect">Gaddafi</a>, it was resolved that the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/African_Union_Commission" title="African Union Commission">African Union Commission</a> would become the <a href="/w/index.php?title=African_Union_Authority&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="African Union Authority (page does not exist)">African Union Authority</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/African_Union#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
