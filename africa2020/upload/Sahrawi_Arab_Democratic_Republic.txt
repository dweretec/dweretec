<p>The <b>Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic</b> (<b>SADR</b>) (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span lang="ar" dir="rtl" xml:lang="ar">الجمهورية العربية الصحراوية الديمقراطية</span>‎ <i><span title="Arabic transliteration" class="Unicode" style="white-space:normal; text-decoration: none">al-Jumhūrīyah al-‘Arabīyah aṣ-Ṣaḥrāwīyah ad-Dīmuqrāṭīyah</span></i>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <span lang="es" xml:lang="es"><i>República Árabe Saharaui Democrática</i></span>) is a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_states_with_limited_recognition" title="List of states with limited recognition">partially recognized state</a> that controls a thin strip of area in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Sahara" title="Western Sahara">Western Sahara</a> region and claims sovereignty over the entire territory of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Sahara" title="Western Sahara">Western Sahara</a>, a former <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spanish</a> colony. SADR was proclaimed by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polisario_Front" title="Polisario Front">Polisario Front</a> on February 27, 1976, in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bir_Lehlou" title="Bir Lehlou">Bir Lehlou</a>, Western Sahara. The SADR government controls about 20–25% of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Territory_(country_subdivision)" title="Territory (country subdivision)">territory</a> it claims.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sahrawi_Arab_Democratic_Republic#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> It calls the territories under its control the Liberated Territories or the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_Zone_(region)" title="Free Zone (region)">Free Zone</a>. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morocco" title="Morocco">Morocco</a> controls and administers the rest of the disputed territory and calls these lands its <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Provinces" title="Southern Provinces">Southern Provinces</a>. The SADR government considers the Moroccan-held territory to be occupied territory, while Morocco considers the much smaller SADR-held territory to be a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buffer_zone" title="Buffer zone">buffer zone</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup></p>
