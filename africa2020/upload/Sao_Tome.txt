<p><b>São Tomé</b> (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> 56,166 in 2005) is the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city" class="mw-redirect">capital city</a> of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Tom%C3%A9_and_Pr%C3%ADncipe" title="São Tomé and Príncipe">São Tomé and Príncipe</a> and is by far that nation's largest town. Its name is <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">Portuguese</a> for "<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_(Apostle)" title="Thomas (Apostle)" class="mw-redirect">Saint Thomas</a>".</p>
