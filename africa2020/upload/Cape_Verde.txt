<p>The <b>Republic of Cape Verde</b> (pronounced <span title="Pronunciation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_English" title="Wikipedia:IPA for English">/ˌkeɪp ˈvɜ:rd/</a></span><small>&#160;(<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Media:Cape_Verde.ogg" title="Media:Cape Verde.ogg"><img alt="Speaker Icon.svg" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Speaker_Icon.svg/13px-Speaker_Icon.svg.png" width="13" height="13" /></a> <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/02/Cape_Verde.ogg" class="internal" title="Cape Verde.ogg">listen</a>)</small>; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">Portuguese</a>: <span lang="pt" xml:lang="pt"><i>Cabo Verde</i></span>, <small>pronounced&#160;</small><span title="Pronunciation in IPA" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_Portuguese" title="Wikipedia:IPA for Portuguese">[ˈkabu ˈveɾdɨ]</a></span>) is an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Island_country" title="Island country">island country</a>, spanning an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Archipelago" title="Archipelago">archipelago</a> located in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Macaronesia" title="Macaronesia">Macaronesia</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ecoregion" title="Ecoregion">ecoregion</a> of the North <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a>, off the western coast of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>, opposite <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mauritania" title="Mauritania">Mauritania</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senegal" title="Senegal">Senegal</a>.</p>
<p>It is slightly more than 4,000&#160;km² in area with an estimated population of over 500,000. The capital of Cape Verde is <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Praia" title="Praia">Praia</a>. The previously <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desert_island" title="Desert island">uninhabited islands</a> were discovered and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colony" title="Colony">colonized</a> by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portuguese</a> in the 15th century, and attained <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Independence" title="Independence">independence</a> from Portugal in 1975.</p>
<p>As of 2007, Cape Verde is classified as a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Developing_country" title="Developing country">developing country</a> after being promoted from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Least_Developed_Countries" title="Least Developed Countries">Least Developed Countries</a> status. About 20% of the population lives on less than $1.25 (U.S.) a day.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cape_Verde#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
