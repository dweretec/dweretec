<p>The <b>Umayyad conquest of North Africa</b> continued the century of rapid <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arab" title="Arab" class="mw-redirect">Arab</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muslim" title="Muslim">Muslim</a> expansion following the death of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muhammad" title="Muhammad">Muhammad</a> in 632 CE. By 640 the Arabs controlled <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mesopotamia" title="Mesopotamia">Mesopotamia</a>, had invaded <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Armenia" title="Armenia">Armenia</a>, and were concluding their conquest of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Byzantine_Empire" title="Byzantine Empire">Byzantine</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syria" title="Syria">Syria</a>. <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damascus" title="Damascus">Damascus</a> was the seat of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umayyad" title="Umayyad" class="mw-redirect">Umayyad</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caliphate" title="Caliphate">caliphate</a>. And by the end of 641 all of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> was in Arab hands. Then, with the destruction of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persia" title="Persia" class="mw-redirect">Persian</a> army at the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Nihaw%C4%81nd" title="Battle of Nihawānd" class="mw-redirect">Battle of Nihawānd</a> (Nehawand) in 642, the conquest of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persian_Empire" title="Persian Empire" class="mw-redirect">Persian Empire</a> was essentially finished.</p>
<p>It was at this point that Arab military expeditions into <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Africa" title="North Africa">North Africa</a> were first launched by local initiative from Egypt, continuing for years and resulting in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spread_of_Islam" title="Spread of Islam">spread of Islam</a>.</p>
<p>In <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/644" title="644">644</a> at Damascus, Caliph <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Umar" title="Umar">Umar</a> (Omar) was succeeded by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uthman_ibn_Affan" title="Uthman ibn Affan">Uthman ibn Affan</a> (Othman), during whose twelve-year rule Armenia, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyprus" title="Cyprus">Cyprus</a>, and all of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>, would be added to the growing Islamic empire; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan" title="Afghanistan">Afghanistan</a> and North Africa would receive major invasions; and Muslim sea raids would range from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rhodes" title="Rhodes">Rhodes</a> to the southern coasts of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iberian_Peninsula" title="Iberian Peninsula">Iberian Peninsula</a>. The Byzantine navy would be defeated in the eastern <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean" title="Mediterranean" class="mw-redirect">Mediterranean</a>.</p>
