<p><b>Tech</b> may refer to:</p>
<ul>
<li>an abbreviation for <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Technology" title="Technology">technology</a>, and terms that are derived from it
<ul>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Technical_support" title="Technical support">technical support</a></li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Techno_music" title="Techno music" class="mw-redirect">techno music</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Technical_problem" title="Technical problem" class="mw-redirect">technical problem</a>, in airline jargon e.g. "the plane's gone tech"</li>
<li>a technician e.g. "we may need to call in a tech"</li>
<li>Tech 2 (or Tech II), an electronic <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/On-Board_Diagnostics" title="On-Board Diagnostics" class="mw-redirect">On-Board Diagnostics</a> tool for General Motors cars</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tech_N9ne" title="Tech N9ne">Tech N9ne</a>, a rapper from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kansas_City" title="Kansas City">Kansas City</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Missouri" title="Missouri">Missouri</a>.</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Progressive_metal" title="Progressive metal">Tech metal</a> a subgenre of Heavy Metal</li>
<li>The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tech_River" title="Tech River" class="mw-redirect">Tech River</a> in France</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghettotech" title="Ghettotech">Ghettotech</a>, the genre of Miami bass-techno fusion</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tech_Mo%27r" title="Tech Mo'r" class="mw-redirect">Tech Mo'r</a>, a member of <i>Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes</i></li>
<li><a href="/w/index.php?title=Tech_Gash&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tech Gash (page does not exist)">Tech Gash</a>, also called <i>"Rape"</i>or <i>"Darkstep"</i>, a sub genre of drum and bass music</li>
<li>In Real-time strategy games, the process of researching a higher <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tier_(Warcraft)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tier (Warcraft) (page does not exist)">Tier</a></li>
<li>A nickname for the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Church_of_Scientology" title="Church of Scientology">Church of Scientology</a>'s teachings, dubbed <i>Scientology Technology</i>; also referred to as <i>LRH (L. Ron Hubbard) technology</i> as well</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Tech</b> is used as a nickname for many <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University" title="University">universities</a>, including:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Institute_of_Technology" title="California Institute of Technology">California Institute of Technology</a>, also called <i>"Caltech"</i>, a university in Pasadena, California</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georgia_Institute_of_Technology" title="Georgia Institute of Technology">Georgia Institute of Technology</a>, also called <i>"Georgia Tech"</i>, a university in Atlanta, Georgia</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illinois_Institute_of_Technology" title="Illinois Institute of Technology">Illinois Institute of Technology</a>, also called <i>"Illinois Tech"</i>, a university in Chicago, Illinois</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louisiana_Tech_University" title="Louisiana Tech University">Louisiana Tech University</a>, also called <i>"Louisiana Tech"</i>, a university in Ruston, Louisiana</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massachusetts_Institute_of_Technology" title="Massachusetts Institute of Technology">Massachusetts Institute of Technology</a>, also called <i>"MIT"</i>, a university in Cambridge, Massachusetts</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Mexico_Institute_of_Mining_and_Technology" title="New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology">New Mexico Institute of Mining and Technology</a>, also called <i>"NMTech"</i>, a university in Socorro, New Mexico</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northwestern_University" title="Northwestern University">Northwestern University</a>'s <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Technological_Institute" title="Technological Institute">Technological Institute</a></li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas_Tech_University" title="Texas Tech University">Texas Tech University</a>, also called <i>"Texas Tech"</i>, a university in Lubbock, Texas</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tunbridge_Wells_Grammar_School_for_Boys" title="Tunbridge Wells Grammar School for Boys">Tunbridge Wells Grammar School for Boys</a> in Royal Tunbridge Wells, Kent, England (a politically incorrect reference to the school's previous guise)</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virginia_Polytechnic_Institute_and_State_University" title="Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University">Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University</a>, also called <i>"Virginia Tech"</i>, a university in Blacksburg, Virginia</li>
<li>The term TeCH is also used in Northern Ireland to refer to Institutes for Further and Higher Education (IFHE). (See; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belfast_Institute_for_Further_and_Higher_Education" title="Belfast Institute for Further and Higher Education" class="mw-redirect">Belfast Institute for Further and Higher Education</a>)</li>
<li><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brooklyn_Technical_High_School" title="Brooklyn Technical High School">Brooklyn Technical High School</a></li>
</ul>
