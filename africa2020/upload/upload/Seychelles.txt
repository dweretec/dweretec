<p><b>Seychelles</b> (pronounced <span title="Pronunciation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_English" title="Wikipedia:IPA for English">/seɪˈʃɛl/</a></span> or <small>IPA:&#160;</small><span title="Pronunciation in IPA" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA" title="Wikipedia:IPA">[/seɪˈʃɛlz/]</a></span> in English and <span title="Pronunciation in IPA" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_French" title="Wikipedia:IPA for French">[seʃɛl]</a></span> in French), officially the <b>Republic of Seychelles</b> (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>: <span lang="fr" xml:lang="fr"><i>République des Seychelles</i></span>; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seychellois_Creole" title="Seychellois Creole">Creole</a>: <i>Repiblik Sesel</i>), is an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Archipelago" title="Archipelago">archipelago</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Country" title="Country">nation</a> of 115 islands in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">Indian Ocean</a>, some 1,500&#160;kilometres (932&#160;mi) east of mainland <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a>, northeast of the island of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">Madagascar</a>. Other nearby island countries and territories include <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zanzibar" title="Zanzibar">Zanzibar</a> to the west, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mauritius" title="Mauritius">Mauritius</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/R%C3%A9union" title="Réunion">Réunion</a> to the south, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comoros" title="Comoros">Comoros</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mayotte" title="Mayotte">Mayotte</a> to the southwest, and the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maldives" title="Maldives">Maldives</a> to the northeast. The Seychelles has the smallest population of any African state <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_countries_by_population" class="external autonumber" rel="nofollow">[1]</a>, <a href="http://goafrica.about.com/od/africatraveltips/a/africafacts.htm" class="external autonumber" rel="nofollow">[2]</a></p>
