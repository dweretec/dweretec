<p>The <b>Republic of Uganda</b> (pronounced <span title="Pronunciation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_English" title="Wikipedia:IPA for English">/juËËÉ¡Ã¦ndÉ/</a></span> or <span title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA">/juËËÉ¡ÉËndÉ/</span>) is a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Landlocked_country" title="Landlocked country">landlocked country</a> in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Africa" title="East Africa">East Africa</a>. It is bordered on the east by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kenya" title="Kenya">Kenya</a>, on the north by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan" title="Sudan">Sudan</a>, on the west by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Democratic Republic of the Congo">Democratic Republic of the Congo</a>, on the southwest by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwanda" title="Rwanda">Rwanda</a>, and on the south by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tanzania" title="Tanzania">Tanzania</a>. The southern part of the country includes a substantial portion of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lake_Victoria" title="Lake Victoria">Lake Victoria</a>, which is also bordered by Kenya and Tanzania.</p>
<p>Uganda takes its name from the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buganda" title="Buganda">Buganda</a> kingdom, which encompassed a portion of the south of the country including the capital <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kampala" title="Kampala">Kampala</a>. Half of the population of the country lives below the international poverty line of US$1.25 a day.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uganda#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
