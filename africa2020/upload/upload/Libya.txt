<p><b>Libya</b> (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span lang="ar" xml:lang="ar"><b><big>ليبيا</big></b></span> ‎ <span lang="ar-Latn" xml:lang="ar-Latn">Lībiyā</span> <span class="unicode" style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ar-Libya.ogg" title="Ar-Libya.ogg"><img alt="Ar-Libya.ogg" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/11px-Loudspeaker.svg.png" width="11" height="11" /></a> <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/d3/Ar-Libya.ogg" class="internal" title="Ar-Libya.ogg">pronunciation</a> <small class="metadata audiolinkinfo" style="cursor:help;">(<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Media_help" title="Wikipedia:Media help"><span style="cursor:help;">help</span></a>·<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ar-Libya.ogg" title="File:Ar-Libya.ogg"><span style="cursor:help;">info</span></a>)</small></span>; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libyan_Arabic" title="Libyan Arabic">Libyan vernacular</a>: Lībya <span class="unicode" style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ar-ly-Libya.ogg" title="Ar-ly-Libya.ogg"><img alt="Ar-ly-Libya.ogg" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/11px-Loudspeaker.svg.png" width="11" height="11" /></a> <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/95/Ar-ly-Libya.ogg" class="internal" title="Ar-ly-Libya.ogg">pronunciation</a> <small class="metadata audiolinkinfo" style="cursor:help;">(<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Media_help" title="Wikipedia:Media help"><span style="cursor:help;">help</span></a>·<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ar-ly-Libya.ogg" title="File:Ar-ly-Libya.ogg"><span style="cursor:help;">info</span></a>)</small></span>; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berber_languages" title="Berber languages">Amazigh</a>: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Libya_amazigh.svg" class="image" title="ⵍⵉⴱⵢⴰ"><img alt="ⵍⵉⴱⵢⴰ" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/e/e6/Libya_amazigh.svg/40px-Libya_amazigh.svg.png" width="40" height="15" /></a>), officially the <b>Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya</b> (&#160; <span lang="ar" dir="rtl" xml:lang="ar"><big><i>الجماهيرية العربية الليبية الشعبية الإشتراكية العظمى</i></big></span> ‎ <span lang="ar-Latn" xml:lang="ar-Latn">Al-Jamāhīriyyah al-ʿArabiyyah al-Lībiyyah aš-Šaʿbiyyah al-Ištirākiyyah al-ʿUẓmā</span> <span class="unicode" style="white-space:nowrap;"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Al-Jamahiriyyah_al-Arabiyyah_al-Libiyyah_ash-shabiyyah_al-Ishtirakiyyah_al-Udhma.ogg" title="Al-Jamahiriyyah al-Arabiyyah al-Libiyyah ash-shabiyyah al-Ishtirakiyyah al-Udhma.ogg"><img alt="Al-Jamahiriyyah al-Arabiyyah al-Libiyyah ash-shabiyyah al-Ishtirakiyyah al-Udhma.ogg" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Loudspeaker.svg/11px-Loudspeaker.svg.png" width="11" height="11" /></a> <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2a/Al-Jamahiriyyah_al-Arabiyyah_al-Libiyyah_ash-shabiyyah_al-Ishtirakiyyah_al-Udhma.ogg" class="internal" title="Al-Jamahiriyyah al-Arabiyyah al-Libiyyah ash-shabiyyah al-Ishtirakiyyah al-Udhma.ogg">pronunciation</a> <small class="metadata audiolinkinfo" style="cursor:help;">(<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Media_help" title="Wikipedia:Media help"><span style="cursor:help;">help</span></a>·<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Al-Jamahiriyyah_al-Arabiyyah_al-Libiyyah_ash-shabiyyah_al-Ishtirakiyyah_al-Udhma.ogg" title="File:Al-Jamahiriyyah al-Arabiyyah al-Libiyyah ash-shabiyyah al-Ishtirakiyyah al-Udhma.ogg"><span style="cursor:help;">info</span></a>)</small></span>), is a country located in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Africa" title="North Africa">North Africa</a>. Bordering the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean_Sea" title="Mediterranean Sea">Mediterranean Sea</a> to the north, Libya lies between <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a> to the east, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudan" title="Sudan">Sudan</a> to the southeast, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chad" title="Chad">Chad</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Niger" title="Niger">Niger</a> to the south, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">Algeria</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tunisia" title="Tunisia">Tunisia</a> to the west.</p>
<p>With an area of almost 1,800,000&#160;square kilometres (694,984&#160;sq&#160;mi), 90% of which is desert, Libya is the fourth largest country in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">Africa</a> by area, and the 17th <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_countries_and_outlying_territories_by_total_area" title="List of countries and outlying territories by total area">largest in the world</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_(political)" title="Capital (political)">capital</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tripoli" title="Tripoli">Tripoli</a>, is home to 1.7 million of Libya's 5.7 million people. The three traditional parts of the country are <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tripolitania" title="Tripolitania">Tripolitania</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fezzan" title="Fezzan">Fezzan</a>, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cyrenaica" title="Cyrenaica">Cyrenaica</a>. Libya has the second highest <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gross_domestic_product" title="Gross domestic product">GDP</a> (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Purchasing_power_parity" title="Purchasing power parity">PPP</a>) per capita of Africa, behind <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equatorial_Guinea" title="Equatorial Guinea">Equatorial Guinea</a>. This is largely due to its large <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petroleum" title="Petroleum">petroleum</a> reserves and low population.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flag_of_Libya" title="Flag of Libya">flag of Libya</a> consists of a <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green" title="Green">green</a> field with no other characteristics. It is the only <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_flag" title="National flag">national flag</a> in the world with just one color and no design, insignia, or other details.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Libya#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
