<p><b>U</b> is the twenty-first letter in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Basic_modern_Latin_alphabet" title="Basic modern Latin alphabet">basic modern Latin alphabet</a>. Its name in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> (pronounced <span title="Pronunciation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_English" title="Wikipedia:IPA for English">/ˈjuː/</a></span>) is spelled <b>u</b>;<sup id="cite_ref-0" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U#cite_note-0"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> the plural is <i>ues,</i> though this is rare.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
