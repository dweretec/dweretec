<p>The <b>Cable News Network</b> (<b>CNN</b>) is an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cable_television" title="Cable television">basic cable</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satellite_television" title="Satellite television">satellite television</a> channel that is owned by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turner_Broadcasting_System" title="Turner Broadcasting System">Turner Broadcasting System</a> division of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_Warner" title="Time Warner">Time Warner</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_cable_news" title="United States cable news">24-hour cable news</a> channel was founded in 1980 by American <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Media_proprietor" title="Media proprietor">media proprietor</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ted_Turner" title="Ted Turner">Ted Turner</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> Upon its launch, CNN was the first television channel to provide <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/24-hour_news_cycle" title="24-hour news cycle">24-hour</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Television_news" title="Television news" class="mw-redirect">news</a> coverage,<sup id="cite_ref-CNNtaipei_4-0" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-CNNtaipei-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> and was the first all-news television channel in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-CNN20_5-0" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-CNN20-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>While the news channel has numerous <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Affiliate" title="Affiliate" class="mw-redirect">affiliates</a>, CNN primarily broadcasts from the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_Warner_Center" title="Time Warner Center">Time Warner Center</a> in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>, and studios in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington,_D.C." title="Washington, D.C.">Washington, D.C.</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, its headquarters at the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN_Center" title="CNN Center">CNN Center</a> in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlanta" title="Atlanta">Atlanta</a> is only used for weekend programming. CNN is sometimes referred to as <b>CNN/U.S.</b> to distinguish the American channel from its international sister network, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN_International" title="CNN International">CNN International</a>. As of August 2010, CNN is available in over 100 million U.S. households.<sup id="cite_ref-CNNdeal_6-0" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-CNNdeal-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup> Broadcast coverage of the U.S. channel extends to over 890,000 American hotel rooms,<sup id="cite_ref-CNNdeal_6-1" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-CNNdeal-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup> as well as carriage on cable and satellite providers throughout <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. Globally, CNN programming airs through CNN International, which can be seen by viewers in over 212 countries and territories.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>As of February 2015, CNN is available to approximately 96,289,000 cable, satellite and, telco television households (82.7% of households with at least one television set) in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNN#cite_note-8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
