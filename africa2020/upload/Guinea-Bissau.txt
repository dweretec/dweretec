<p>The <b>Republic of Guinea-Bissau</b> (pronounced <span title="Pronunciation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_English" title="Wikipedia:IPA for English">/ˈɡɪni bɪˈsaʊ/</a></span>; <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">Portuguese</a>: <span lang="pt" xml:lang="pt"><i>República da Guiné-Bissau</i></span>, <small>pronounced&#160;</small><span title="Pronunciation in IPA" class="IPA"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:IPA_for_Portuguese" title="Wikipedia:IPA for Portuguese">[ʁɛˈpublikɐ dɐ ɡiˈnɛ biˈsau]</a></span>) is a country in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Africa" title="Western Africa" class="mw-redirect">western Africa</a>, and one of the smallest states in continental Africa. It is bordered by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senegal" title="Senegal">Senegal</a> to the north, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinea" title="Guinea">Guinea</a> to the south and east, with the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> to its west.</p>
<p>Its size is nearly 37,000&#160;square kilometres (14,000&#160;sq&#160;mi) with an estimated population of 1,600,000. Formerly the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portuguese</a> <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colony" title="Colony">colony</a> of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portuguese_Guinea" title="Portuguese Guinea">Portuguese Guinea</a>, upon independence, the name of its capital, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bissau" title="Bissau">Bissau</a>, was added to the country's name to prevent confusion with the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinea" title="Guinea">Republic of Guinea</a>. Guinea-Bissau's <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GDP_per_capita" title="GDP per capita" class="mw-redirect">GDP per capita</a> is one of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_countries_by_GDP_(PPP)_per_capita" title="List of countries by GDP (PPP) per capita">lowest in the world</a>.</p>
