The name <i>Lesotho</i> translates roughly into <i>the land of the people who speak <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sotho_language" title="Sotho language">Sesotho</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lesotho#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> About 40% of the population lives below the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poverty_threshold" title="Poverty threshold">international poverty line</a> of US $1.25 a day.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lesotho#cite_note-6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
