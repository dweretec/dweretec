<p><b>Senegal</b> (<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/French_language" title="French language">French</a>: <span lang="fr" xml:lang="fr"><i>le Sénégal</i></span>), officially the <b>Republic of Senegal</b>, is a country south of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/S%C3%A9n%C3%A9gal_River" title="Sénégal River">Sénégal River</a> in western Africa. Senegal is bounded by the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic Ocean</a> to the west, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mauritania" title="Mauritania">Mauritania</a> to the north, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mali" title="Mali">Mali</a> to the east, and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinea" title="Guinea">Guinea</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinea-Bissau" title="Guinea-Bissau">Guinea-Bissau</a> to the south, and it also encircles <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Gambia" title="The Gambia">The Gambia</a> on its three sides, except that of the Atlantic Ocean.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senegal#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>Its size is almost 197,000&#160;km² with an estimated population of about 12.5 million.</p>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dakar" title="Dakar">Dakar</a> is the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_city" title="Capital city" class="mw-redirect">capital city</a> of Senegal, located on the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cap-Vert" title="Cap-Vert">Cape Verde Peninsula</a> on the country's <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">Atlantic</a> coast. About a third of the population live below the international poverty line of US$ 1.25 a day.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Senegal#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
