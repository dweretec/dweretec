href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seychellois_Creole" title="Seychellois Creole">Creole</a>: <i>Repiblik Sesel</i>), is an <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Archipelago" title="Archipelago">archipelago</a> in the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">Indian Ocean</a>. The 115-<a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Island_country" title="Island country">island country</a>, whose capital is <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victoria,_Seychelles" title="Victoria, Seychelles">Victoria</a>, lies 1,500 kilometres (932&#160;mi) east of mainland <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southeast_Africa" title="Southeast Africa" class="mw-redirect">Southeast Africa</a>. Other nearby island countries and territories include <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zanzibar" title="Zanzibar">Zanzibar</a> to the west and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comoros" title="Comoros">Comoros</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mayotte" title="Mayotte">Mayotte</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">Madagascar</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/R%C3%A9union" title="Réunion">Réunion</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mauritius" title="Mauritius">Mauritius</a> to the south.</p>
<p>Seychelles, with a population of 90,024, has the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_African_countries_by_population" title="List of African countries by population">smallest population of any African state</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seychelles#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup> Seychelles is a member of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/African_Union" title="African Union">African Union</a>.</p>
